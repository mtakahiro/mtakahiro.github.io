# Japanese webpage patch

Copy the files in `_pages/` into your repository's `_pages/` directory.

Then add the entries in `_data/navigation_ja_addition.yml` to `_data/navigation.yml` under `main:`.

The Japanese pages will be available at:

- `/ja/`
- `/ja/cv/`
- `/ja/research/`
- `/ja/publications/`

Each Japanese page includes an English-page link at the top.
